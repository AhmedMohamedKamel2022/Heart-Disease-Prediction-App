Did you know that machine learning models can help you predict heart disease pretty accurately😲!
In this app, you can estimate your probability for having heart disease in seconds 😀. Here, a decision tree model using an over sampling technique was constructed using survey data of over 300k US residents from year 2020.

To predict your heart disease status, simply follow the steps bellow:
- Filling your data that describe you ✍.
- Press "Predict" and wait for the result 💗.





Note: The range of probability for having heart disease between range 0% to 30% with other technique has the same attributes of this technique (random forest) which used in this app.
Therefore do not rely entirely on this result and go to the doctor, whatever the result.
Good Luck 💗.
